About us

Mission

LeafInk’s mission is to provide a practical and environmentally responsible writing instrument by bridging tradition with sustainability. The business wants to enhance the composition of Malunggay and Charcoal ink through continuous research and development, providing dependability, vibrant expressiveness, and a less carbon impact.

Vision

LeafInk’s vision is to redefine the Ink industry, fostering innovation, eco-friendly practices, and a positive impact on individuals and communities. The business aims to be a catalyst for progress, where every Malunggay ink contributes to a more sustainable and prosperous future.
